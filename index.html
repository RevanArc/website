The Duality of Security

Like the legendary Revan who mastered both the light and dark sides of the Force, RevanArc embodies the dual nature of AI security. We unite offensive and defensive capabilities, bringing together red team adversarial techniques with blue team protective measures.

Our mission is to advance the state of AI security through open research, practical tools, and collaborative knowledge sharing across the entire security spectrum.
